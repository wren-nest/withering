The TV Room

At first the only thing you see are rings of light. Or they appear to be rings, they wrap around cylindrical objects- your mind fills in the part you cannot see.

You notice other objects about the room amoung the pylons. A couch, a table, an armchair.

They all seem unfamiliar and strange in the strange sideways light that fills the room.
