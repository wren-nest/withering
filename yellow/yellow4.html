The TV Room

At first the only thing you see are rings of light. Or they appear to be rings, they wrap around cylindrical objects- your mind fills in the part you cannot see.

You notice other objects about the room amoung the pylons. A couch, a table, an armchair.

They all seem unfamiliar and strange in the strange sideways light that fills the room.

Your shadow is obscuring the door behind you that you just came through or- no, when you move the darkness stays still. Or at least, it doesn't move as you do.

You watch as the Nothing slowly creeps back out under the door.



You sit in the armchair for a moment.
On the carpeted floor in front of you is a tv, one of those old, square shaped ones. It isn't plugged in.


You're starting to get a headache, your temples throbbing. You blame it on the weird lighting.



There is only one way forward from here, a door at the end of the room. You could go back through the door you entered through- the one the Nothing left through.
In fact, the door looks quite different now, it might lead somewhere new...

You clear that thought from your mind quickly.

Which way will you go?
